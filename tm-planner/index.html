 Help
IMPORTANT
  •   Team saves ONLY LOCALLY
  •   Clearing your browser cache will delete your saves
  •   If you want to view your teams in a difference device, you should Export your Teams and paste the link into the browser of the other device
USAGE
  •   Drag & Drop Units into the slots below, or Click to Add using the add buttons to assign available Units
  •   Remove Units from teams by dragging it outside, or Click and select Remove
  •   Units cannot be used more than once, except in the Friend Captain slot or the Ambush Team (Team 6) (These Units are tinted to distinguish from the rest)
  •   The Captain slot will be automatically mirrored to the Friend Captain slot if it is empty
  •   The Captain slot should be assigned first before assigning the Friend Captain slot to allow using the same unit.
NOTABLE FEATURES
  •   FILTER
    Filters are available by toggling the Filter button. Units can be filtered by Type, Class, and Special
  •   UNIT DETAIL
    Clicking on the Units will display the details: Captain, Special, etc
  •   MINI GUIDE
    Click on the boss name to open its guide. Adjusting the Nav Lv will update the HP and ATK at the corresponding nav level. Clicking the button next to the boss actions will activate the corresponding Special Filter
  •   UNITS I DON'T HAVE
    Label Units you don't have by mouse right click (or long hold on touch devices), or by dragging them to the Don't Have Units section
  •   Check out the News page for more features and their details!
